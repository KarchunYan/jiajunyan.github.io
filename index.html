About Me

Jiajun Yan

Jiajun Yan is about to finish his studies at Howard Community College. His major is AVMP (Audio Video Media Production). Before that, he had many years of work experience in Guangzhou, China. He worked in the sales team of Yuexiu Real Estate for 5 years and became one of the “100-million-yuan Sales” in 2014 due to his outstanding sales performance. Later, Jiajun joined Guangzhou WinMax Property Agency with his extensive sales experience. During this period, he was exposed to media production and served as corporate communications executive. He was responsible for company poster production, trophy customization, interviews, event shooting and video production.

After arriving in the United States, Jiajun helped TekLab School produce promotional videos during the pandemic. In addition, he went to HCC to improve his English and media production skills. Due to his studies in the United States, he can speak three languages: Mandarin, Cantonese and English. In his spare time, Jun also enjoys traveling and photography.
